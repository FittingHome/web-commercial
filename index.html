Key dates of our project 2020-2022

The objective open our mind and make us think about subjects and societal/economic problems as opposed to strictly technical problems. with Identify problems, and “difficult” issues within societal topics, demonstrate the ability to apply solutions to problems encountered, make the connection between a current societal topic and the solutions that I.T. can provide and 1 minute and 30 seconds to pitch about the problem we've highlighted and our solution to it.

After having learned the approach of a project of this scale thanks to the moonshot, we had brainstormed on an idea of project which we liked and which seemed relevant. We had made the remark during this brainstorming that it would be interesting to be able to try clothes on online sales websites from home before buying them, it would avoid the return of parcels because the size or the clothes don't suit us and to be more satisfied with our purchases as we don't need to go to try them on.

Once the idea was found, we had to develop it between us and have a much more viable, desirable and feasible approach to our project (how to achieve it, at what cost, with the help of whom) and then we had to pitch it and have it validated for our pedagogical signatories.

The idea of the forward is to make a sprint of 2 weeks by advancing on the idea of the project and by proving its feasibility and its viability, to have a first return and to refine the next sprint By having at the end of the sprint a demo to return of what could resemble at long term the project, and to train us to follow a rhythm of sprint and review, the validation of this sprint is a key moment as for the future of this idea of project which could become concrete.

The beta of our project will consist of putting the project online and having it tested by users before finalizing the project.

App User

This unique application allows you to try on clothes on a 3D avatar that suits you. You can turn around the model and make it do animations !

Simple design and quick use.

- Start by 3D scanning yourself from one of our points of sale or your phone.


- Once the scan is done, we will send you the qr code to load your 3d avatar to the app.


- Your 3D avatar is finally ready! Dress him up to try on lots of outfits and see if you match the styles you're trying on.


- You can even save your outfits and find them in the "wardrobe" section !

No more waiting line at the fitting rooms.

- You will be able to wear several clothes at the same time (Up/Down).


- You can turn around your avatar by sliding my finger on the screen.


- And even change the zoom by pinching the screen with my fingers.


- You can then from the sites of our partners, put aside the articles that you like the most to try them on our application.


- Mix the articles of the different sites from your wardrobe!

Scan

Follow our instructions to create a 3d scan of yourself, be careful during the scan.

Scaniverse

Use the Scaniverse app to scan yourself. Stay still in the T Pose position during the scan. Ask someone to scan you. Export your model in .obj.

Meshmixer

Fix holes and smooth your model with Meshmixer. If your model still is ready start a new scan. Follow the link to help you.

Mixamo

Create an account on Mixamo and upload your model. Use auto rigger of Mixamo and export your model in .glb or .gltf
